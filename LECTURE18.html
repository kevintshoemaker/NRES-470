Upcoming final exam

when and where The final exam is coming up on May 14 (9:50am to 11:50 in our regular classroom). You will have the whole two hour period to take the exam (but you should not need the whole period).

what The exam will cover all material covered in lectures and lab.

  • All material in Chapters 1-6 of the Gotelli book
    • Exponential pop growth
    • Density-dependent pop growth
    • Age-structured pop growth
    • Metapopulations (additional suggested reading: Griffin et al. 2008)
    • Competition
    • Predation
  • Systems thinking and modeling (Ch. 1, 2, and 10 of “Beyond Connecting the Dots”)
  • Individual-based models (IBM) (Ch. 10 of “Beyond Connecting the Dots”).
  • Basic computer programming (e.g., if-then-else statements)
  • Application of matrix population models to conservation and management (including Heppell 1998)
  • Stochasticity and Uncertainty, including Lande 1992, Regan 2002 and all material covered in lecture and lab.
  • Small-population paradigm and declining-population paradigm (including Beissinger and Westphal 1998, Caughley 1988)
  • Population Viability Analysis, in theory and practice
  • Parameter estimation for PVA (including basic capture-recapture methods) (helpful reading: Amstrup et al)

The exam will consist of a mixture of multiple-choice and short-answer questions.
